# Company Return Policy

## Overview
Our company offers a flexible 30-day return policy for most products.

## Eligibility
- Products must be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Items must be in original condition with tags attached
- Proof of purchase required (receipt or order number)

## Process
1. Contact customer service to initiate return
2. Receive return authorization number
3. Ship item back using provided label
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

## Exceptions
- Final sale items cannot be returned
- Custom or personalized items are non-returnable
- Software and digital products are non-returnable once opened

## Refund Method
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item was defective

For questions about returns, contact:
- Email: returns@company.com
- Phone: 1-800-RETURNS
- Hours: Monday-Friday 9am-5pm EST
